Discover the Pearl of Africa

Uganda is a breathtaking land of stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and incredible biodiversity. Known as the "Pearl of Africa," we offer unparalleled experiences, from the source of the Nile to mountain gorilla tracking in Bwindi.

About Uganda Tourism

Definition of Tourism: Tourism involves travel for pleasure or business; it encompasses the activities of people traveling to and staying in places outside their usual environment for not more than one consecutive year for leisure, business, and other purposes.

Impact of COVID-19 on the Tourism Sector

The COVID-19 pandemic severely impacted Uganda's tourism sector, leading to travel bans, border closures, and a significant loss of revenue. This affected tour operators, accommodation facilities, and conservation efforts.

Solutions Implemented

  • Promotion of **domestic tourism** through special packages.
  • Implementation of **Strict Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** for traveler safety.
  • Digital marketing campaigns to **maintain visibility** for future travel.
